5 Toilet Cleaning Mistakes You're Probably Making

Team Klenzo 12 May 2026 4 min read
Home/ Blog/ 5 Toilet Cleaning Mistakes You're Probably Making

A spotless toilet looks simple to maintain, but a handful of everyday habits quietly work against you. Here are the five mistakes we see most often — and the easy fixes for each.

Skipping the under-rim: Most of the bacteria and limescale build-up happens where you can't see it. Always angle the nozzle under the rim and let the cleaner sit for a few minutes before scrubbing.

Using too much product: More cleaner doesn't mean more clean — it just means more rinsing. A thin, even coat left to dwell for 3–5 minutes outperforms a flooded bowl every time.

Scrubbing before the cleaner has worked: Klenzo's formula needs a little dwell time to break down grime chemically. Give it a moment before you reach for the brush.

Forgetting the flush handle and seat hinges: These are touched more often than almost anything else in the bathroom, yet rarely wiped down. A quick pass with a cloth after every clean keeps germs from spreading.

Not ventilating after cleaning: Even plant-based formulas work best when the bathroom airs out afterward. Crack a window or run the exhaust fan for 10 minutes post-clean.
