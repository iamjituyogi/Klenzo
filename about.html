Our Story

Two brothers, one kitchen-table idea.

Klenzo began in 2019 with two brothers, a rented lab bench and a shared frustration — every cleaner strong enough to cut grime was harsh on skin, and every "gentle" one barely worked. We believed families shouldn't have to choose.

Three years of formulation later, we launched our first batch of plant-based toilet and floor cleaners out of a small unit in Rajasthan. Today, Klenzo powers over 12,000 homes across India, still run the same way it started — family-first, formula-obsessed.

  • Founded in 2019 by the Yogi brothers
  • Plant-based formulas, lab-tested for real results
  • Proudly built and made in India
Our Purpose

What drives us, every single day.

A mission worth building a company around, and a vision that keeps us formulating better.

Our Mission

To make powerful, plant-based cleaning accessible to every Indian household — without the harsh chemicals, without the compromise, and without the premium price tag.

Our Vision

A future where "clean home" and "safe home" mean the same thing — where every family can trust what's under their sink as much as what's on their plate.

Our Values

Honesty in every ingredient list, respect for the planet in every bottle, and relentless testing so every claim we make, we can prove.
